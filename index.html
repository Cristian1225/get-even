This page helps you transform a sentence into title case. Title case means that the first letter of each word is capitalized, while the rest of the letters are in lowercase. To use this feature, follow these simple steps:

  1. Type your sentence in the input field provided.
  2. Click the "Submit" button.
  3. Wait for a moment, and the converted sentence will appear in the output section below.

With this tool, you can easily format your sentences to have a more professional or polished appearance. Give it a try!
